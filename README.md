### What is SideKickWeb? 
**SideKickWeb** is an AI Text Humanization and AI Text Detection service, designed for writers, students, and content creators.

### How do I use this service?
Using  **SideKickWeb** is *extremely* simple. All you have to do is [**visit our website**](https://sidekickweb.app/try) and paste the content you want to Humanize. 

To access the **Detector and Better Humanization** features, you can **make a free account** to gain access to the **SideKickWeb Dashboard**, where you will be able to **Humanize/Detect your AI content**, and also be able to see your past humanization results.

### This sounds cool, but, how much is all of this?
**SideKickWeb** is (and will forever be) **a free service for anyone to use**!

Free users can humanize their content up to **25 times per hour**, with a max text length of **4,000 characters** per request.

There are paid plans for our service, which **grant higher text limits**, **higher requests per hour**, and **higher queue priority** so you can receive your humanized content quicker.
